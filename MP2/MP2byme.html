Education is the best investment

          For me, education is indeed the best investment a person could have, why? Because having a college degree diploma is not a piece of paper that you can have right away or buy from anywhere… No one can buy a diploma in order to be hired into a certain position in a company or institution. Having education is a thing that you invest almost half of your lifetime! Right when you are five or six years old you will start going to school until you finish your college degree. Imagine how long it takes for you to finish school, how much effort you will exert all through out, how much energy you need to use in order to get through it. You invest a lot of your lifetime studying and pursuing a course for a job that you want someday but after all of those hardships, efforts, and struggles it will be worth it after you finish your degree because you will have a diploma that is needed for you to apply and qualify for a decent job. If you finished school and have a decent degree you will more likely to have a decent a job with a decent salary that will eventually increase if you perform well enough and guess what? Having a good salary will eventually lead you to having a good financial status that will eventually lead you to having a decent life! If you have good salary you can afford the things that you always wanted when you were still studying and buy your parents a house to thank them for everything that they sacrificed for you in order to graduate. So for me Education is basically the best investment because having a diploma would make you more likely to find a decent job!
